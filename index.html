About Us

Caring for Bangalore's Smiles Since 2012

At SmileCare Dental, we believe every patient deserves a comfortable, honest, and affordable dental experience. Our clinic in Koramangala is equipped with digital X-rays, rotary root canal systems, and ultrasonic scalers — the same technology used in the best clinics across India.

Led by Dr. Priya Sharma (MDS, Prosthodontist), our team of specialists treats every patient with patience, empathy, and complete transparency on costs.

  • ✅ Fully sterile equipment after every patient
  • ✅ Completely painless injections using topical anaesthesia
  • ✅ Transparent pricing — no hidden charges ever
  • ✅ Insurance & EMI accepted
